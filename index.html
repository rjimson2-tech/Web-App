Welcome to AutoGrow!

This quick guide will walk you through how to use the system. You can reopen this anytime by clicking the ? Guide button in the top navigation bar.

First, check your sensor cards on the Dashboard. Make sure all readings are showing real values, not 0. If everything reads 0, the hardware may be offline or still booting up.

🎨

Understanding the Dashboard

🟢 Green Readings are within the healthy range. Everything is good.
🟡 Yellow Getting close to the edge. Keep an eye on it — it may need attention soon.
🔴 Red Critical — the reading is outside the safe range and needs attention now.
🟢 Connected to Database The web app is successfully communicating with Firebase. If this turns red, check your internet connection.
⏱️ Hardware: Last seen The last time the ESP32 sent sensor data. If it says OFFLINE, the device may be unplugged or has lost WiFi.
🔧

Test Your Actuators

Before running Auto mode, confirm the hardware is responding by testing in Manual mode.

💨 Vent Fan Toggle it ON and check if the fan physically turns on. The Live status below the button should turn 🟢.
💧 Water Pump Check the zones you want to open, then toggle the pump. Water should flow through the selected zone valves only.
🌿 Grow Lights Toggle it ON and confirm the grow light physically lights up.
🌫️ Mist Maker Toggle it ON and confirm the mist maker starts producing mist.
⚙️

Configure Your Crop

Head to the Setup page using the top navigation.

  1. Pick your crop from the dropdown (Tomato, Lettuce, or Kangkong).
  2. Click Load Defaults to fill in the recommended settings for that crop.
  3. Review and adjust the thresholds if needed.
  4. Set your watering schedule — the times the system will check and water automatically.
  5. Click Save Custom Settings to confirm.
🚀

Start Growing!

You're almost ready. Before starting, complete the Pre-Grow Checklist on the Setup page:

✅ Seeds placed Seeds or seedlings are in the greenhouse.
✅ Soil prepared Soil is ready in all 3 zones.
✅ Water tank filled The water reservoir is filled and ready.

Once all boxes are ticked, click Start Growing. The system will automatically switch to Auto mode and manage watering, lighting, ventilation, and humidity for you.
